# mcrutils: Miscellaneous Utilities for Data Cleaning, Analysis, and Reporting

Convenience functions for data cleaning, analysis and reporting,
including creating and visualizing year-to-date and quarterly analyses,
and determining customer account status for customer churn analysis.
